# New
Battle against other guilds in this new turn based 4x mass battler mode! Plan your strategy together, cooperate and conquer the land!

Since this game mode is a huge addition, please note that there is always the chance of some bugs or issues to arise. To make it up to you preemptively, I'm also releasing a new Gift code!

The new code: `AL888`

Additionally, the game is now playable on even more devices, most notably, Google Play Games for PC and Chromebooks so if you want to have the game for your PC, now is the time!

See you in the fields of glory, legends!
