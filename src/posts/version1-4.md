## Updated
- Change in PvP decay, it will now be a percentage of your current rating that will be subtracted each season end, rather than a flat amount.
- Season rewards for items have moved one rarity up: Item rewards will now be <Rarity rarity="epic" /> & <Rarity rarity="legendary" /> instead of <Rarity rarity="rare" /> & <Rarity rarity="epic" />.

## Balance
#### Druid
- Chain Lightning: Deals `100%` damage to consecutive hits (was `85%` / `50%`)
- Chain Lightning, Greater: Deals `100%` damage to consecutive hits (was `85%` / `50%`)

Druid's Chain Lighting and Chain Lighting, Greater Consecutive lighting hits will now inflict 100% damage instead of 85% and 50%

## Fixes
- Points not awarded in some PvP matches has now been fixed.
