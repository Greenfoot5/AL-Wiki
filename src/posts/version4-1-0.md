## Features and QoL:
### Achievements
There are now google play games achievements (that can be browsed from the "Season & Quests" window)
![Screenshot showing google play games services achievements](/news/gpgsAchievements.png)

### Battle Statistics
On the battle results panel, there is a new button that can toggle on/off the statistics view where you can see how each member of your team and the enemy team performed
![Screenshot showing the battle result panel with statistics](/news/battleStatistics.png)

### Inspect Guild
You can now inspect other Guilds! the Guild Details panel opens either by clicking the Guild's emblem in leaderboard or by clicking the Guild Name in the character inspection panel
![Screenshot showing guild details for another guild](/news/guildDetails.png)

### Required level for Guild Dominion
Guild Dominion now requires a minimum level to join (atm the minimum level is 10 but it can be raised in the future)
![Screenshot showing the Guild Dominion event requiring level 10](/news/guildDominionReqLvl.png)

### Persistent settings
Mass claim options in the mailbox panel will now persist across app restarts
![Screenshot showing the mass claim settings with options ticked](/news/massClaimSettings.png)

### Inactive Guild Commanders
Guild Commanders will be replaced if they are inactive for more than 30 days.
