## New
### Tier 5
This means a whole new map for expeditions to clear, and plenty of new gear & styles to collect!
![Screenshot showing 3 characters in Tier 5 equipment, left to right: light, medium, heavy](/news/tier5.png)

### Item Transmogrification

One much requested feature has made its way to Aeon's Legends, Item Transmogrification
You can now change the appearance of your Armour and  Weapons! The new feature is available in the character screen, when you select an item
![Screenshot displaying the new item transmogrification menu in the character scene](/news/transmog.png)

### Manual Skill Casting
Another much requested feature was the ability to manually cast your skills. Now you can!
The new feature is available in all modes, giving you finer control over your skills and your combos!

![Screenshot from an expedition showing off the new manual casting menu in the bottom right](/news/manualskills.png)

### Festive Decortations
There are some festive decoration now in the hub area, to make you feel a little bit cozy! (also watch out for the fireworks~)
